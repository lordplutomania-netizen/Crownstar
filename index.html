Is forex trading risky?

Yes, forex trading carries high risk. You can lose all your invested capital. That's why we emphasize risk management and only recommend trading with money you can afford to lose.

Do I need prior experience?

Not at all! Our curriculum starts from absolute basics and progresses to advanced strategies. We have students who started with zero knowledge and became profitable traders.

Refund of mentorship fee is valid within one week of subscription. Refund will not be processed if request is made after the first week (5 working days of subscription).
